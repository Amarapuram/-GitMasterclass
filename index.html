About Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players each. It is one of the most popular sports globally, known for its rich history and passionate fan following.

Cricket Rules

Cricket involves two teams taking turns to bat and bowl. Batsmen score runs by hitting the ball, while the bowling team aims to dismiss them. The game consists of innings, overs, and various ways to score and be dismissed.

Cricket Events

Cricket is played at various levels, from local and domestic competitions to international tournaments like the ICC Cricket World Cup, T20 World Cup, and more. These events showcase top teams and players from around the world.
